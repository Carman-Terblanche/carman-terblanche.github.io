Core Features

Everything an agent needs, nothing they don't

Property Management

Add, edit, filter, and sort listings with images, pricing, features, and color-coded cards by type — House, Land, Apartment, Town House.

Contact Management

Track buyers, sellers, renters, and landlords with preferences, budgets, and areas of interest. Import in bulk via CSV.

Automatic Matching

Contacts are auto-matched to properties based on location, type, budget, bedrooms, and features — triggered on every new entry.

WhatsApp Integration

Connect to WhatsApp Business API. Messages auto-create contacts and extract preferences via a built-in NLP inference engine.

Property Scraping

Paste a listing URL to auto-import. Add URLs to a watch list that polls every 10 minutes for new properties with deduplication.

Smart Notifications

Dashboard alerts for new matches, new contacts, overdue follow-ups, and scraped listings — all with one-click navigation.
