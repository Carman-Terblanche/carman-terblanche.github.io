About Me

Creative Visionary

Since a young age, I've been an extremely creatively driven person. Whether it's music, drawing, photography, or anything I could get my hands on.

I eventually studied Computer and Information Sciences at Monash University with a major in Mobile Systems. Although it's an interesting field, I cannot give up my desires to create art.

I created a YouTube channel in 2021 where I made comedy content and honed a lot of my editing skills. I used those skills to make videos for international and local clients.

I am now on the lookout for amazing projects such as music videos, film projects, or social media.
