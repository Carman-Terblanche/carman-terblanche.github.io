About Me

Performing Artist

KRMN (Ka-min) writes, produces and performs his own songs from his bedroom studio. He creates music that speaks about purpose and social commentary with a style that leans into raw instrumentals with Hip Hop, Rock and Electronic influences.

KRMN has always been fascinated by music and songwriting. He started writing songs when he was only 10. When he turned 16, he started channeling his creativity into poetry. His love for art and language only grew stronger when he got introduced to Hip Hop music, coming from primarily listening to Rock genres. His inspiration came from bands like My Chemical Romance and Bring Me The Horizon.

In 2020, writing became his outlet, getting more involved with the Hip Hop genre. He started producing music for the first time in 2021. Some of his current biggest inspirations are Ren and Crywolf. KRMN is a passion project, life goal and the answer to the ultimate question.
